## üìö HIDDEN LEARNING (Educational Mechanics)

Kids are learning while they play:

- **PATTERNS** - Recognizing and replicating sequences (AB, ABC patterns)
- **COUNTING** - Beats are counting practice (1-2-3-4, 1-2-3-4)
- **FRACTIONS** - Long notes vs short notes (whole, half, quarter notes)
- **SEQUENCES** - Order matters in rhythm
- **PREDICTION** - Anticipating the next beat
- **BODY COORDINATION** - Physical learning and motor skills
- **TIMING** - Understanding duration and tempo
- **MEMORY** - Remembering and reproducing patterns

## üéØ DIFFICULTY PROGRESSION

The game naturally scaffolds from simple to complex:

- **Level 1**: Steady single beats (tap-tap-tap-tap)
- **Level 2**: Two alternating patterns (red-blue-red-blue)
- **Level 3**: Varying speeds (fast-fast-slow-slow)
- **Level 4**: Rests and silence (tap-...-tap-...)
- **Level 5**: Complex combinations (rainbow rhythms!)
- **Level 6**: Symphony Station (everything combined!)
