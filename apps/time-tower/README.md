# THE TIME TOWER

A steampunk time machine elevator that teaches Grade 1 students history through cooking adventures.

## Overview

**Concept**: An elevator that travels through TIME instead of space. Each floor is a different era with cooking challenges that combine history and food education.

**Aesthetic**: Steampunk time machine with clockwork gears, spinning cogs, brass mechanisms, and swirling time vortex effects.

## Pilot Experience: Ancient Egypt

### Journey Flow

1. **Time Lobby** - Steampunk lobby with the magnificent Time Elevator
2. **Approach Elevator** - See the brass cage with spinning gears and clock face
3. **Select Era** - Pocket watch buttons - select Ancient Egypt (Floor 2)
4. **Time Travel** - Gears spin, clock hands whirl, vortex swirls!
5. **Arrival** - Doors open to reveal Ancient Egypt - gold, hieroglyphics, warm light
6. **Meet Chef Khepri** - A friendly Egyptian chef greets Isaiah
7. **Read Recipe** - Simple recipe for honey bread (200 words, Grade 1 level)
8. **Cooking Mini-Game** - Measure ingredients with counting (3 flour, 2 honey, 1 water)
9. **Baking** - Watch the bread bake with warm glow animation
10. **Reward** - Pharaoh awards the Golden Scarab badge!

### Educational Content

**Reading Comprehension**:
- 200-word story about Ancient Egyptian honey bread
- Grade 1 reading level
- Historical facts integrated into narrative

**Math Skills**:
- Counting and measuring ingredients
- Simple addition (3 + 2 + 1 = 6 measurements)
- One-to-one correspondence

**History & Culture**:
- Ancient Egypt lived 5,000 years ago
- Egyptians were first bread bakers
- Nile River importance
- Pharaohs and royal customs
- Honey's value in ancient times

**Science**:
- Heat makes bread rise
- Ingredients mixing to create new substances
- Observation of changes (color, texture)
