# Quick Launch Guide

## To Start the Ocean Floor Express:

### Option 1: Direct Browser
Simply double-click `index.html` to open in your default browser.

### Option 2: From Terminal
```bash
cd /Users/charleswu/Desktop/+/home_school/isaiah_school/apps/ocean-express
open index.html
```

### Option 3: Local Server (Recommended)
```bash
cd /Users/charleswu/Desktop/+/home_school/isaiah_school/apps/ocean-express
python3 -m http.server 8080
```

## The Pilot Experience

When you launch, you'll experience:

1. **Surface Dock** - See the brass bathysphere at the pier
2. **Click "Enter the Bathysphere"** - Step inside the vessel
3. **Pull the Lever** - Begin your descent into the ocean
4. **Sunlight Zone (200m)** - Learn about dolphins
5. **Question Gate** - Answer to descend deeper
6. **Twilight Zone (1000m)** - Discover bioluminescent jellyfish
7. **Midnight Zone (4000m)** - Meet the glowing anglerfish!
8. **Earn Your Badge** - Complete the discovery
9. **Return to Surface** - Celebrate your achievement

## What to Look For

### Visual Elements
- Brass porthole with rivets and bolts
- Rotating depth gauge needle
- Filling pressure gauge
- Bubbles rising past the window
- Swimming creatures at each depth
- Glowing bioluminescent effects

### Animations
- Water caustics (light patterns in shallow water)
- Bubble rise (continuous bubbling)
- Creature drift (fish swimming by)
- Bioluminescent pulse (glowing creatures)
- Anglerfish lantern (the iconic glowing lure!)
- Badge glow (celebration effect)

### Interactive Elements
- Descent lever (brass handle to pull)
- Story panels (read about each zone)
- Question gate (comprehension check)
- Answer buttons (multiple choice)
- Return button (after earning badge)

## Educational Value

Isaiah will learn about:
- Ocean zones and their characteristics
- Dolphins as air-breathing mammals
- Bioluminescence in deep-sea creatures
- The anglerfish and its hunting adaptations
- Pressure and depth relationships
- Marine biology concepts

## Tips for Best Experience

- Use a tablet or desktop for full effect
- Turn up brightness to see bioluminescence glow
- Read the stories carefully for comprehension questions
- Take time to watch creatures swim by at each depth
- Notice how the ocean gets darker as you descend
- Look for the anglerfish's glowing lantern in the Midnight Zone!
