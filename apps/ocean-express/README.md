# Ocean Floor Express - Bathysphere Adventure

A Grade 1 educational app that takes Isaiah on a deep-sea journey through the ocean's mysterious zones in a vintage Jules Verne-inspired bathysphere.

## Aesthetic: Bioluminescent Art Nouveau

The app combines steampunk elegance with deep-sea bioluminescence:
- **Color Palette**: Abyss black, deep navy, bioluminescent cyan/magenta, amber brass
- **Typography**: Cinzel Decorative (headers), Lora (body text)
- **Style**: Art Nouveau curves, brass porthole frames, glowing sea creatures

## The Journey

### Surface Dock
- Beautiful pier with the brass bathysphere waiting
- Enter through the brass door to begin your descent

### Bathysphere Interior
- **Depth Gauge**: Watch the needle rotate from 0m to 11,000m
- **Pressure Gauge**: See pressure increase as you descend
- **Main Porthole**: Large brass-framed window showing the ocean
- **Descent Lever**: Pull the brass lever to go deeper

### The 5 Ocean Zones

1. **Sunlight Zone (0-200m)**
   - Bright, colorful water with dolphins, fish, and coral
   - Learn about dolphins as mammals that breathe air

2. **Twilight Zone (200-1000m)**
   - Dimming blue light, jellyfish with bioluminescence
   - Discover how creatures make their own light

3. **Midnight Zone (1000-4000m)**
   - Complete darkness with glowing anglerfish
   - Read the amazing anglerfish story (200 words)
   - See its glowing lantern lure

4. **Abyssal Zone (4000-6000m)**
   - Strange pale creatures near thermal vents
   - Extreme pressure environment

5. **Hadal Zone (6000m+)**
   - The deepest trenches on Earth
   - Mysterious and alien world

## Educational Mechanics

### Reading Comprehension
- Stories about ocean creatures at each depth
- Learn scientific facts through narrative
- Grade 1 appropriate vocabulary

### Question Gates
- Answer comprehension questions to descend deeper
- Multiple choice format
- Immediate feedback (correct/incorrect)

### Discovery Badge
- Complete the journey to earn the "Glowing Anglerfish Badge"
- Celebrates learning achievement
- Return to surface to explore again

## Animations

### CSS Keyframes
- `bioluminescent-pulse`: Creatures glowing in darkness
- `bubble-rise`: Bubbles floating up past porthole
- `creature-drift`: Fish and jellyfish swimming by
- `depth-gauge-move`: Needle rotating with depth
- `pressure-increase`: Gauge filling with pressure
- `anglerfish-lantern`: The iconic glowing lure
- `water-caustics`: Light patterns in sunlight zone
- `badge-glow-pulse`: Discovery badge celebration

## Files

- **index.html**: Complete standalone app structure
- **styles.css**: Bioluminescent Art Nouveau styling (all animations)
- **app.js**: Bathysphere mechanics, descent system, creature animations
- **pilot-story.js**: Educational stories for each zone

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Click "Enter the Bathysphere" at the surface dock
3. Pull the brass descent lever to descend
4. Read the story at each zone
5. Answer the comprehension question to continue
6. Discover the anglerfish in the Midnight Zone
7. Earn your badge and return to surface
