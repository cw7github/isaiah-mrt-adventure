# THE GROWTH TOWER

**A Living Building Where Plants Tell Stories**

An educational Grade 1 app teaching the plant life cycle through an organic, interactive adventure inspired by William Morris botanical patterns and Studio Ghibli animation aesthetics.

---

## QUICK START

Open `/apps/growth-tower/index.html` in a web browser. The app is fully self-contained and requires no build process or server.

---

## CONCEPT

The Growth Tower is a magical building where a giant vine serves as the elevator, carrying you through five floors that represent stages of plant growth. Each floor teaches science concepts while a storytelling sunflower shares its journey from seed to bloom.
